Aliwal Shoal Break Away

Friday, 24 - Sunday, 26 Aug

Quick breakaway from the Joburg Rat Race?

A quick 6 hour drive gets you to the closest beach from Johannesburg. What does this get you? SHARKS! and WRECKS! Aliwal Shoal offers the adventure junkie diver amazing Shark dives or world class Wrecks. For the more chilled, you can go and explore the prestine reefs. We simply park the cars, kit up and stroll down to the beach. 

As a Club we stay in the quaint little town of Scottburgh which boasts with one of the most popular sheltered bathing beaches in South Africa. The town itself offers an array of restaurants and beach shops you can easily walk to.
Making this the perfect family getaway!
